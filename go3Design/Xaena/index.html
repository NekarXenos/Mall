Introducing the Xaena Rotary Exotic Car

Imagine the power of a Le Mans winning four-rotor engine coupled with the torque of electric motors. Package that into a design inspired by the beautiful Furai and you have a unique exotic car. With more grunt than a boxer. Revving smoothly over 9000rpm with the greatest of ease. And it turns heads. Contact us if you want to help make it happen.

The Mission:

To produce bespoke mid-engine 4-rotor exotic super-cars

The Vision:

To give the rotary engine the honour it deserves by using it to power a high end exotic super-car before all vehicle are forced to be electric.
